Home Curry protein shake
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 egg
  • milk
  • 1 scoop chia seeds
  • 2 scoops oats
  • banana
  • 2 tbsp greek yoghurt

Steps

  1. Add oats
  2. Add greek yoghurt
  3. Add a scoop of protein powder
  4. Crack an egg and add it
  5. Squeeze honey into it
  6. add chia seeds
  7. break banana into pieces then add it
  8. Pour milk into it
  9. Enjoy Yaar!
