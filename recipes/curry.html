Home Curry protein shake
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • half tsp haldi
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 skinless boneless chicken
  • garlic
  • 3 tbsp oil (can be olive, vegetable or sunflower)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Saute onion until lightly browned
  2. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar, and salt. Continue stirring for 2 mins
  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yoghurt, and coconut milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 mins.
  4. Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more mins.
  5. Enjoy yaar!
